Content modes

What are content modes?

Adding multiple content examples to every UI element in your design system can help designers and writers craft
content quickly and efficiently for any use case – but it can also be heavy and hard to maintain. The new Figma variables
provide an easy and convenient solution to this problem. Check out the empty state example below to see how you can
incorporate 4 different content examples into a single UI component.
